Streamlined Data Analytics



Tools built for easy data analytics deployment solving 2 main issues : Lack of IT resources and/or High IT constraints.

Lack of IT resources: No programming language (fully) available on your machine ? IT enviromnent not enough mature to deploy data solutions ? No proper dataviz tools and don't want to pay hefty prices from mainstream tools that are either too restricted or too complex for your needs ?

High IT constraints: Cannot send sensitive data in the cloud ? Cannot develop solutions with your favourite language due to external packages you cannot install ? Your project is not the IT department priority yet you are tied to its strict policies ?


Client Side Solutions answers your needs with the following benefits:

  • Safe by design: Everything stay on your machine. You don't believe us ? Visit the demo pages and ... shutdown your internet connection. (You will need to reactivate your connection each time you load another demo page).
  • Pragmatic by purpose: Having trimmed the fat and the unnecessary to comply with "challenging IT environment" our solutions are lightweight and can be used during POC/Discovery phases as well as in production. Hence faster implementation for small data projects.
  • Tailored by choices: Different tools for different tasks. We built modular solutions that can be combined to fulfill your needs. And we can easily develop customized solutions as our tools are not tied with any third party apps / licenses.
