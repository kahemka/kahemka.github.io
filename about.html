Manifesto: Purposes & Motivations

  • What

    What is CSS ?

    CSS: Client Side Solutions is a personal project that aims to provide simple yet powerful tools regarding the analytical space.

    You will find on this website tools & services that can be used right away. Play with it and explore the capabilites of CSS. A documentation is available here for more informations about the various displayed apps.

    Keep it mind that these showcased applications were meant to tackle predetermined tasks for demonstration purpose. Therefore, they can be tuned to better fit similar needs you might have.

  • Why

    Why CSS

    Client Side Solutions : Working on the client side.

    Server based/online/cloud solutions are convenient to sell : You take your customer's data and you keep the code.

    Howevever, there are several issues for the customer: the end product does not entirely belong him. If interested, he is forced to subscribe and pay recurrent fees. And if he unsubscribes, back to square one with another solution.

    Another issue: these solutions are not meant for everyone.

    Indeed, server based solutions might not be allowed in some IT environment : issue regarding sending sensitive data, not enough IT maturity or lack of programming skills.

    Last but not least: While the server based approach enables complex analytic operations, it is often overkill regarding the targeted task. Wasting energy and unecessary ressources:

  • How

    How does CSS work ?

    Client Side Solutions : Working on client side.

    Put it simply : when you are visiting a website, your computer is doing some work to render what you are seeing. Client Side Solutions just ask him on top of that, a little extra-work to perform specific tasks instead of requesting cloud ressources.

    For tasks showcased here : no need to spend more energy powering up distant servers to achieve what is needed. No need to send (sensitive) data far away: everything stay in your computer.

    And you keep the code. Your data is safe and the code fully auditable.

  • Who

    For who is CSS designed ?

    People looking for simple & tailored solutions as the one that are showcased here.

    People with limited IT ressources : either because of strict IT constraints or lack of IT ressources.

    Hobbyists who wants to play with data and simple analytic solutions without having to spend time installing lots of dependancies / creating an account for a cloud service they won't use 2 month later.
